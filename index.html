About Me

Apart from drifting aimlessly from interest to interest, I can be found outside if I'm lucky. I think caves are super cool and have been fortunate enough to briefly work with the Cave Pearl Project, developing a cheap(er) DIY light sensor to place in Yucatan cenotes. I consider myself an amateur photographer but still surprise myself when I take better pictures than the auto setting on the camera. One day, I hope to summit Mt. Rainier and Kilimanjaro, that would be real cool. While in college I wrote for the university's satire publication and, once I've gathered the motivation to follow through, hope to (one day) actually publish some writing - maybe even make money off it.
